What is Threat?

A potential cause of an incident that may result in harm to a system or organization Example: In a system that allows weak passwords

  • Threat:
    An intruder can exploit the password weakness to break into the system
  • Threat Agent:
    Entities that would knowingly seek to manifest a threat
